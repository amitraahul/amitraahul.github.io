About Me

I am an experimental particle physicist working on electron–positron collider data from the BESIII experiment at BEPCII, IHEP (Beijing). My research focuses on searches for physics beyond the Standard Model using charmonium decays, invisible final states, and rare baryon processes.

I am currently affiliated with Chung-Ang University, Seoul, and collaborate closely with international groups within the BESIII collaboration.

Research Interests

  • Searches for dark matter and invisible decays at e+e− colliders
  • Charmonium and bottomonium decays
  • Heavy Neutral Leptons and neutrino portals
  • Baryon number violation and exotic baryon decays
  • Statistical analysis, maximum-likelihood fitting, and machine learning in HEP
